THREE SIGNALS ONLY THE M-PESA RAIL CAN SHIP

3 default fields Rail-side derivation MSISDN-grounded Telco-fused

Underwrite a borrower on rail-level identity — identity

Identity confidence grounded in MSISDN, GSM, and device signal. KYC-grade decision in 60 seconds.

Read the fields
Fields returned on every C2B, B2C, and Status response

confidence (0–1), device_match (bool), location_ok (bool), risk (enum: low, medium, high, blocked).

Rail-side inputs the score is grounded on

MSISDN registration age, SIM-swap window, device fingerprint, GSM Header Enrichment, location consistency against transaction history. Telco signal fused with transaction graph at a density structurally unique to M-Pesa in Africa.

Products your code can ship today

Microloan disbursement in 60 seconds. KYC-embedded account opening. Pre-STK fraud gating on P2P transfers. Tiered onboarding that skips manual review when confidence is high. Any flow where identity was previously the blocker.

Build AI products on consented cash rhythm — intelligence

Derived cash-flow patterns behind scope-limited, revocable consent. Signal that doesn't need surveillance.

Read the fields
Fields returned behind a scope-limited consent

rhythm (enum: weekly, bi-weekly, monthly, irregular), next_low (predicted date), categories[] (spend segments), churn_risk (0–1), spend_segment (decile).

Consent model

OAuth-style, scope-limited, time-boxed, revocable from the customer's phone. Downstream calls after revocation return consent: revoked — your app degrades gracefully without silent failure.

Data boundary

Derived patterns only. The transactions behind the rhythm stay on the rail. Your app builds on the shape of cash flow; customers keep the trust they already place in M-Pesa.

Products your code can ship today

AI financial companions that guide customers before cash runs short. Pre-empty nudges and personalized savings flows. Credit terms priced on cash-flow shape rather than balance. Subscription hygiene and category-level planning on inferred patterns.

Price 3M+ merchants on live two-sided visibility — merchant_health

Categorical health read computed across every merchant on M-Pesa. Live on every B2C and C2B.

Read the fields
Categories returned on every B2C and C2B response

status (stable / growing / declining / at_risk), trend (MoM delta), risk_flag (null or categorical).

Factor indicators

Transaction velocity trend (rolling 30-day), days-active ratio, reversal rate, merchant-category cohort variance. Computed rail-side with two-sided transaction visibility across 3M+ merchants — signal density no external platform in Africa can assemble.

Default availability

Attached to every B2C and C2B response by default. No separate endpoint. No per-query pricing. No additional consent surface — the signal describes the merchant your transaction is already touching.

Products your code can ship today

Merchant financing priced on a live two-sided signal. Tiered supplier terms (30-day on growing, cash-on-delivery on at_risk). Same-day merchant onboarding. Category-level portfolio risk. Dynamic settlement scheduling.

DATA STAYS ON THE RAIL

Your code gets the decision. Safaricom keeps the raw.

Every field on this page is derived at rail level. Your app receives what it needs to branch — a confidence score, a category, a predicted date — not the data behind it. The customer's trust in M-Pesa stays intact. Your product gets to build on it.

  • Derived signals only intelligence.rhythm is a pattern, not the transactions that formed it. merchant_health.status is a category, not the velocity trend behind it. Raw history never leaves Safaricom.
  • Consent where it matters intelligence requires OAuth-style, scope-limited, revocable consent granted from the customer's phone. identity and merchant_health attach to transactions you are already authorized to process — no additional consent surface required.
  • Revocation is immediate After a customer revokes, the very next call returns consent: revoked. Your app degrades gracefully; no silent failure, no stale signal.
  • PII sanitization on the SDK Phone numbers masked by default (254708374149 becomes 254708***149) in logs, MCP tool outputs, and anything that might enter an AI agent context.
  • Reconcilable audit Every intelligence field on a response traces back to the rail call that produced it. You ship the decision. Safaricom holds the receipt.
